The story

Meet Steve

How the world found out about him.

Steve Tucker is 83. He's spent a lifetime stopping wherever the world looked interesting and drawing it — in ink, on paper, right there. He taught art in Terre Haute his whole life, made courtroom sketches, and has been recording the world around him for decades.

Matt Stamper ran into him at Spring Mill Lodge in Indiana. They got talking, Matt got to know the sketches, photographed the sketchbooks, and shared them on X. The thread went everywhere. Matt then decided to help Steve put his work on the Tezos blockchain — and they've been finding their homes ever since.
